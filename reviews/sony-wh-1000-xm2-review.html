# SONY WH-1000-XM2 Review

For more details watch the SONY WH-1000-XM2 video Review

Terrible Name…Sony cmon you can find better names for your headphones lineup. Despite the less happy name, in this category (over ear noise cancelling headphones) this ones are the real deal and I will explain why later. These headphones have great sound, one of the best noise cancellation and they are very comfortable.

Sony wh-1000-xm2 is available to buy here:

The unboxing experience is top of the line, you feel that you are in the presence of a expensive product with attention to details.

These headphones come with a good hard case like most headphones but the way they fold is really annoying, you need to fold the right ear cup in order to get them fit in the case.

The case itself is fine and also has a small place in the back to store the the charging cable for example.

About by the sound quality, these headphones sound excellent, they are full rich and clear with the sound they have. For example you have more range and more deep base then the bose qc35, even the noise cancellation is slightly better.

# Confort

In terms of confort, I can guarantee that only after long hours of use will you realize that you are using a pair of headphones. They are very comfort and lightweight. The design is excelent, the ear cups have a good amount of padding and the top band is padded well. They are plenty flexible enough to not be squeezing your head all the time and getting uncomfortable.

In terms of isolation, they do a pretty well job. the good insulation of the ambient sound along with the noise cancellation feature and the great sound quality make these headphones unbeatable.

# Controls

In terms of controls, there are only 2 physical buttons, all the rest of the controls is all done by swipping and tapping on the right ear cup which has a small touchpad. Tap to play and pause, swipping up and down to change the volume, and forward and backward to change the tracks. To be honest to me I would prefer so have physical buttons or a dial like the microsoft surface headphones, because the touch controls do not work every time but you get used to it.

Back to the physical buttons, there are two, the power button, and the noise cancellation / (slash) ambient sound switch. The power button is also used to pair the headphones, you hold the button for some seconds and then you are able to connect them to a new device.

The battery life is enough to last for almost a week without being charged, it lives up to around 30 hours, and with just 10 minutes of charge you get more than 1 hour of play time.

The left ear cup has an NFC logo on, that can be used to tap to pair on the side of the headphones with an NFC enable device. This is a great feature but this headphones only pair to one device at a time. It means for example that every time you want to switch from your mobile phone to your laptop you need to pair again which is a bit annoying.

It’s also possible to use the mobile app to to optimize the noise cancellation feature, you can switch between different sound profiles or you can costumize everything using the equaliser.

# Best deal in this category

Now I will tell you why I consider this ones the best deal in this category. I bought these ones to my wife on the last black friday for around one hundred and seventy euros which was less then half the price of the new xm3, which are now listed for 3 hundred and eighty euros.

The big differences from xm3 to xm2, are the redesign ear cups with a little more padding which increase the isolation from the environment sound, the top band also has a bigger padding And off course the usb-c input instead of the micro usb.

Apart from this everything is similar, I advice you to check more technical reviews about the sound quality and noise cancellation performance if need more detail information.

# Nice to have

I defiantly would like to see on the xm4s, the next of version of this ones:

  • ability to pair with multiple devices
  • the ability to detect when you took them off your head and they pause and when you put them back on, they would keep playing again, like the surface headphones,

# Conclusion

In conclusion I loved this headphones, the design and build quality is top of the line:

  • how they look is great,
  • how they sound is great,
  • how they feel is great,

They are excellent for those who fly frequently or for those who work in a noisy environment.
